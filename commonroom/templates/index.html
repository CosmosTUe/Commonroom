Reservation

The Common Room can be booked by any association for students that wants to host an event. Events can make use of our facilities, such as a beamer, dining tables and a kitchen. To make sure we can facilitate everything we only accept reservation requests made 7 days in advance.

For reservation of the Common Room, you can fill in the reservation form. Please make sure to have read the Common Room regulations before submitting your form. Know that the Common Room is kept open by volunteers and we may not be able to keep the room open at all times!
